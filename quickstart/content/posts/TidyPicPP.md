## Introduction

TidyPic ("we," "our," or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Photo Library Access
- **What We Access**: TidyPic requires access to your photo library to scan for screenshots, videos, duplicate photos, and similar photos.
- **How We Use It**: All photo scanning and analysis is performed **locally on your device**. We do not upload, store, or transmit your photos to any external servers.
- **User Control**: You can revoke photo library access at any time through your device's Settings > Privacy > Photos.

### 2. Device Storage Information
- We collect information about your device's storage capacity and usage to display storage statistics.
- This data is processed locally and is not transmitted to external servers.

### 3. In-App Purchase Information
- When you purchase the Premium version, Apple processes the transaction through their secure payment system.
- We do not collect or store your payment information.
- We only receive a verification that the purchase was successful.

### 4. Analytics and Crash Reports (Optional)
- We may collect anonymous usage statistics and crash reports to improve app performance.
- This information does not include any personal data or photos.
- You can opt out of analytics in your device settings.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:
- Identify and help you delete duplicate or similar photos
- Display storage and memory usage statistics
- Provide premium features to paying users
- Improve app performance and fix bugs

## Data Storage and Security

- **Local Processing**: All photo analysis is performed **locally on your device**.
- **No Cloud Storage**: We do not upload or store your photos on any external servers.
- **No Third-Party Access**: Your photos are never shared with third parties.
- **Secure Deletion**: When you delete photos through TidyPic, they are permanently removed from your device according to iOS system policies.

## Third-Party Services

TidyPic uses the following Apple services:
- **StoreKit**: For processing in-app purchases
- **PhotoKit**: For accessing your photo library
- **WidgetKit**: For displaying home screen widgets

These are Apple's native frameworks and follow Apple's privacy policies.

## Children's Privacy

TidyPic does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access**: View what data the app uses (locally on your device)
- **Deletion**: Delete the app and all associated data at any time
- **Opt-Out**: Disable photo library access in device settings
- **Contact**: Reach out with privacy concerns

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:
- Posting the new Privacy Policy in the app
- Updating the "Last Updated" date

## Data Retention

- We do not retain any of your photos or personal data.
- All processing is done locally on your device.
- Deleting the app removes all associated data.

## International Users

TidyPic is available worldwide. By using the app, you consent to the processing of your information as described in this Privacy Policy, regardless of your location.

## Consent

By using TidyPic, you consent to this Privacy Policy.

# TidyPic（净图）隐私政策

## 引言

TidyPic（净图，以下简称"我们"、"本应用"）致力于保护您的隐私。本隐私政策说明了您在使用我们的移动应用程序时，我们如何处理您的信息。

## 我们收集的信息

### 1. 照片库访问权限
- **访问内容**：TidyPic 需要访问您的照片库，以扫描截图、视频、重复照片和相似照片。
- **使用方式**：所有照片扫描和分析均在**您的设备本地**完成。我们不会上传、存储或传输您的照片到任何外部服务器。
- **用户控制**：您可以随时通过设备的"设置 > 隐私 > 照片"撤销照片库访问权限。

### 2. 设备存储信息
- 我们会收集您设备的存储容量和使用情况信息，以显示存储统计数据。
- 这些数据在本地处理，不会传输到外部服务器。

### 3. 应用内购买信息
- 当您购买高级版时，Apple 通过其安全支付系统处理交易。
- 我们不收集或存储您的支付信息。
- 我们仅收到购买成功的验证信息。

### 4. 分析和崩溃报告（可选）
- 我们可能会收集匿名使用统计数据和崩溃报告，以改进应用性能。
- 这些信息不包含任何个人数据或照片。

## 数据存储和安全
