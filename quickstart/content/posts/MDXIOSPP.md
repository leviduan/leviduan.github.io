## Introduction

MDX Reader ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our application.

## Information We Collect

### What We DON'T Collect

MDX Reader is designed with privacy in mind. We **DO NOT**:

- Collect any personal information
- Track your usage or behavior
- Access your contacts, location, or photos
- Use analytics or tracking services
- Share any data with third parties
- Require account creation or login
- Connect to external servers

### What We Store Locally

All data is stored **locally on your device only**:

- **Dictionary Files**: MDX/MDD dictionary files you import
- **Search History**: Words you've looked up (stored locally)
- **Favorites**: Words you've bookmarked (stored locally)
- **App Settings**: Your preferences (font size, theme, etc.)

## How We Use Your Information

All data remains on your device and is used solely to:

- Display dictionary definitions
- Remember your search history for quick access
- Save your favorite words
- Apply your personalized settings

## Data Storage & Security

- All data is stored in your device's local storage
- No cloud synchronization or backup occurs
- Data is protected by your device's security features
- You can delete all app data at any time through Settings

## Third-Party Services

MDX Reader **does not use** any third-party services, including:

- Analytics platforms (e.g., Google Analytics, Firebase)
- Advertising networks
- Social media integrations
- Cloud storage services
- Crash reporting tools

## Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

- **Access**: View all locally stored data within the app
- **Delete**: Clear search history, favorites, or all data through Settings
- **Export**: Dictionary files remain accessible in your device's file system
- **Uninstall**: Deleting the app removes all associated data

## Children's Privacy

MDX Reader does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13. The app is safe for all ages.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Changes will be reflected with a new "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

## Data Portability

Your dictionary files and data remain in standard formats:
- **MDX/MDD files**: Industry-standard dictionary format
- **SQLite database**: Standard database format for history and favorites

You can back up or transfer these files independently.

✅ **Local storage only** - All data stays on your device

## 简介

MDX Reader（"我们"、"本应用"）致力于保护您的隐私。本隐私政策说明我们在您使用本应用时如何处理您的信息。

## 我们收集的信息

### 我们不收集的信息

MDX Reader 采用隐私优先的设计理念。我们**不会**：

- 收集任何个人信息
- 追踪您的使用情况或行为
- 访问您的联系人、位置或照片
- 使用分析或追踪服务
- 与第三方共享数据
- 要求账户创建或登录
- 连接到外部服务器

### 我们本地存储的内容

所有数据**仅存储在您的设备上**：

- **词典文件**：您导入的 MDX/MDD 词典文件
- **搜索历史**：您查找过的单词（本地存储）
- **收藏夹**：您收藏的单词（本地存储）
- **应用设置**：您的偏好设置（字体大小、主题等）

## 我们如何使用您的信息

所有数据都保存在您的设备上，仅用于：

- 显示词典定义
- 记住您的搜索历史以便快速访问
- 保存您收藏的单词
- 应用您个性化的设置

## 数据存储与安全

- 所有数据都存储在您设备的本地存储中
- 不进行云同步或备份
- 数据受您设备的安全功能保护
- 您可以随时通过设置删除所有应用数据

## 第三方服务

MDX Reader **不使用**任何第三方服务，包括：

- 分析平台（如 Google Analytics、Firebase）
- 广告网络
- 社交媒体集成
- 云存储服务
- 崩溃报告工具

## 您的权利

您拥有对数据的完全控制权：

- **访问权**：在应用内查看所有本地存储的数据
- **删除权**：通过设置清除搜索历史、收藏夹或所有数据
- **导出权**：词典文件在您设备的文件系统中保持可访问
- **注销权**：删除应用会移除所有关联数据

## 儿童隐私

MDX Reader 不收集任何人（包括 13 岁以下儿童）的任何个人信息。本应用对所有年龄段都是安全的。

## 政策变更

我们可能会不时更新本隐私政策。变更将反映在新的"最后更新"日期中。在变更后继续使用本应用即表示您接受更新后的政策。

您的词典文件和数据采用标准格式：
