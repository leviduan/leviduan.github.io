# Privacy Policy for MusiPaper

**Last Updated:** January 6, 2026

## Introduction

MusiPaper ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our iOS application.

## Information We Collect

### Information We DO NOT Collect

MusiPaper is designed with privacy in mind. We **do not** collect, store, or transmit any of the following:

- ❌ Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
- ❌ Location data
- ❌ Usage analytics or behavioral data
- ❌ Device information or identifiers
- ❌ Contacts, photos, or other personal files
- ❌ Browsing history or search queries

### Information Stored Locally on Your Device

The following information is stored **only on your device** and never leaves your iPhone/iPad:

- ✅ **Purchase Status**: Whether you have unlocked the Pro version (stored via Apple's StoreKit)
- ✅ **Save Count**: Daily wallpaper save count for free tier users (stored in UserDefaults)
- ✅ **Last Reset Date**: The date when the daily save count was last reset

This local data is:
- Not accessible to us or any third parties
- Automatically deleted if you uninstall the app
- Not backed up to iCloud (by default)

## How We Use Your Information

Since we don't collect any personal information, there is nothing for us to use or share.

The app operates entirely on your device, using:
- **iTunes Search API**: To fetch album artwork and music information from Apple's public catalog
- **Photo Library Access**: To save wallpapers to your device (only when you explicitly tap "Save")

## Third-Party Services

### Apple Services

MusiPaper uses the following Apple services:

1. **iTunes Search API**
   - Purpose: Fetch album artwork and metadata
   - Data Shared: Search queries (e.g., artist names)
   - Privacy: Queries are sent to Apple's servers but are not associated with your identity
   - Policy: [Apple Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/)

2. **StoreKit (In-App Purchase)**
   - Purpose: Process Pro version purchases
   - Data Shared: Purchase transaction data (handled entirely by Apple)
   - Privacy: We never see your payment information
   - Policy: [Apple IAP Privacy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/)

3. **Photo Library (Optional)**
   - Purpose: Save wallpapers to your device
   - Access: Only when you explicitly tap "Save Wallpaper"
   - Privacy: We cannot access your existing photos; we can only save new images

### No Third-Party Analytics or Advertising

We **do not** use:
- ❌ Google Analytics, Firebase, or any analytics services
- ❌ Facebook SDK or social media tracking
- ❌ Advertising networks (AdMob, etc.)
- ❌ Crash reporting tools that collect personal data

## Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device:
- Your data is protected by your device's built-in security (passcode, Face ID, Touch ID)
- We have no servers, so there's no risk of data breaches from our side
- Your purchase status is synced via Apple's secure infrastructure

## Children's Privacy

MusiPaper does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13. Since we don't collect any data, the app is safe for all ages.

## Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

- **Delete Your Data**: Uninstall the app to remove all local data
- **Reset Purchase Status**: Use the "Restore Purchase" feature in the upgrade prompt
- **Revoke Photo Access**: Go to iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Photos → MusiPaper → Remove Access

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:
- Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top
- Displaying an in-app notice (for significant changes)

## Transparency Commitment

We believe in radical transparency:
- ✅ This app is built to respect your privacy
- ✅ We will never sell or share your data (because we don't have any)
- ✅ Future updates will maintain the same privacy-first approach

---

**Summary:** MusiPaper does not collect, store, or share any personal information. Everything happens on your device. Your privacy is our priority.

# MusiPaper 隐私政策

**最后更新日期：** 2026年1月6日

## 简介

MusiPaper（"我们"或"本应用"）致力于保护您的隐私。本隐私政策说明了我们在您使用本 iOS 应用程序时如何处理信息。

## 我们收集的信息

### 我们不会收集的信息

MusiPaper 在设计时充分考虑了隐私保护。我们**不会**收集、存储或传输以下任何信息：

- ❌ 个人身份信息（姓名、邮箱、电话号码）
- ❌ 位置数据
- ❌ 使用分析或行为数据
- ❌ 设备信息或标识符
- ❌ 通讯录、照片或其他个人文件
- ❌ 浏览历史或搜索记录

### 本地存储在您设备上的信息

以下信息**仅存储在您的设备上**，绝不会离开您的 iPhone/iPad：

- ✅ **购买状态**：您是否已解锁专业版（通过 Apple StoreKit 存储）
- ✅ **保存次数**：免费版用户的每日壁纸保存次数（存储在 UserDefaults）
- ✅ **上次重置日期**：每日保存次数上次重置的日期

这些本地数据：
- 我们或任何第三方无法访问
- 卸载应用后自动删除
- 默认不备份到 iCloud

## 我们如何使用您的信息

由于我们不收集任何个人信息，因此没有任何信息可供使用或分享。

本应用完全在您的设备上运行，使用：
- **iTunes Search API**：从 Apple 公开目录获取专辑封面和音乐信息
- **相册访问权限**：将壁纸保存到您的设备（仅在您明确点击"保存"时）

## 第三方服务

### Apple 服务

MusiPaper 使用以下 Apple 服务：

1. **iTunes Search API**
   - 用途：获取专辑封面和元数据
   - 共享数据：搜索查询（例如艺术家名称）
   - 隐私：查询发送到 Apple 服务器，但不与您的身份关联
   - 政策：[Apple 隐私政策](https://www.apple.com/cn/legal/privacy/)

2. **StoreKit（应用内购买）**
   - 用途：处理专业版购买
   - 共享数据：购买交易数据（完全由 Apple 处理）
   - 隐私：我们绝不会看到您的支付信息
   - 政策：[Apple IAP 隐私](https://www.apple.com/cn/legal/privacy/)

3. **相册（可选）**
   - 用途：将壁纸保存到您的设备
   - 访问：仅在您明确点击"保存壁纸"时
   - 隐私：我们无法访问您的现有照片；只能保存新图片

### 无第三方分析或广告

我们**不使用**：
- ❌ Google Analytics、Firebase 或任何分析服务
- ❌ Facebook SDK 或社交媒体跟踪
- ❌ 广告网络（AdMob 等）
- ❌ 收集个人数据的崩溃报告工具

## 数据安全

由于所有数据都存储在您的设备本地：
- 您的数据受设备内置安全保护（密码、Face ID、Touch ID）
- 我们没有服务器，因此不存在我们这边的数据泄露风险
- 您的购买状态通过 Apple 的安全基础设施同步

## 儿童隐私

MusiPaper 不会有意收集任何人的个人信息，包括 13 岁以下儿童。由于我们不收集任何数据，本应用对所有年龄段都是安全的。

## 您的权利

您对自己的数据拥有完全控制权：

- **删除您的数据**：卸载应用即可删除所有本地数据
- **重置购买状态**：在升级提示中使用"恢复购买"功能
- **撤销相册访问**：前往 iOS 设置 → 隐私与安全性 → 照片 → MusiPaper → 移除访问权限

## 本政策的变更

我们可能会不时更新本隐私政策。我们将通过以下方式通知您任何变更：
- 更新顶部的"最后更新日期"
- 显示应用内通知（针对重大变更）

## 透明承诺

我们相信彻底的透明度：
- ✅ 本应用旨在尊重您的隐私
- ✅ 我们绝不会出售或分享您的数据（因为我们根本没有）
- ✅ 未来的更新将保持同样的隐私优先方法

---

**总结：** MusiPaper 不收集、存储或共享任何个人信息。一切都在您的设备上进行。您的隐私是我们的首要任务。
