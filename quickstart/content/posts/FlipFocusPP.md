# 1. English

## Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: December 10, 2025**

### Introduction

Welcome to FlipFocus ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application FlipFocus (the "App").

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

---

### Information We Collect

#### Data Collected by FlipFocus

FlipFocus does not collect any personal data directly. We do not collect, store, or transmit:

- Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
- Location data
- Contacts or address book information
- Photos or media files
- Health or fitness data
- Financial information
- User-generated content

#### Data Collected by Third-Party Services

However, third-party advertising services integrated into our App may collect certain information automatically. Specifically, **Google AdMob** may collect the following data:

| Data Type | Purpose | Collected By |
|-----------|---------|--------------|
| Device Identifier (IDFA) | Personalized advertising, cross-app ad tracking, ad performance measurement | Google AdMob |
| IP Address | Fraud prevention, approximate location for ad targeting, analytics | Google AdMob |
| Device Information | Ad optimization, device compatibility | Google AdMob |
| App Usage Data | Analytics, ad frequency capping | Google AdMob |

**Important**: Device identifiers may be used to deliver personalized ads across apps and websites, and to measure advertising performance. This data is collected and processed by Google, not by FlipFocus.

#### Information Stored Locally

The following data is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to any server:

- App settings and preferences
- Timer configurations
- Display customization options

---

### How Your Information Is Used

The data collected by third-party services is used for the following purposes:

1. **Advertising**: To display relevant advertisements within the App
2. **Cross-App Tracking**: To deliver personalized ads across different apps and measure ad effectiveness
3. **Analytics**: To understand how users interact with the App
4. **Fraud Prevention**: To detect and prevent fraudulent activity

---

### Third-Party Services

Our App integrates with the following third-party services:

#### Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob collects and processes data according to Google's Privacy Policy. This includes:

- **Device Identifiers**: May be used to deliver personalized ads across apps and measure ad performance
- **IP Address**: Collected by Google for fraud prevention and approximate geographic targeting
- **Usage Data**: Collected to optimize ad delivery and frequency

All advertising data is collected, processed, and stored by Google according to their privacy practices. FlipFocus does not have access to or control over this data.

- **Google Privacy Policy**: https://policies.google.com/privacy

#### Apple Services

We integrate with Apple's system features:

- **WidgetKit**: For Home Screen widgets (no data collection)
- **ActivityKit**: For Live Activities (no data collection)

These Apple services do not collect or transmit any personal data.

---

### Advertising and Your Choices

#### Personalized Advertising

By default, ads may be personalized based on your interests and activity across apps. Device identifiers may be used for cross-app advertising tracking.

You can opt out of personalized advertising and tracking:

1. **iOS Settings**: Go to Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking > Turn off "Allow Apps to Request to Track"
2. **In-App Consent**: When prompted by the App Tracking Transparency dialog, tap "Ask App Not to Track"
3. **Reset Advertising Identifier**: Go to Settings > Privacy & Security > Apple Advertising > Reset Advertising Identifier

#### Non-Personalized Ads

If you opt out of tracking, you will still see ads, but they will not be personalized based on your activity.

---

### Children's Privacy

FlipFocus is rated 4+ and is suitable for all ages. However:

- We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age
- We do not share any children's data with third parties for advertising purposes
- The third-party advertising SDK (Google AdMob) is configured to comply with applicable children's privacy laws
- If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child's data has been collected inappropriately, please contact us immediately

We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations worldwide.

---

### Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

- All data transmission uses secure protocols (HTTPS/TLS)
- Local data is stored using iOS secure storage mechanisms
- We do not operate servers that store your personal data
- Third-party services use industry-standard security practices

---

### Data Retention

- **Local Data**: Stored on your device until you delete the App or clear App data
- **Advertising Data**: All collected advertising data is managed by Google AdMob according to their data retention policies. FlipFocus does not retain any advertising data.

---

### Data Storage

FlipFocus does not operate any servers that store user data. All advertising data collected through the App is stored and managed by the respective third-party providers (Google) according to their privacy policies and data retention practices.

### Informazioni che raccogliamo

#### Dati raccolti da FlipFocus

FlipFocus non raccoglie direttamente alcun dato personale. Non raccogliamo, memorizziamo o trasmettiamo:

- Dati sulla posizione
- Informazioni sui contatti o rubrica

- Dati sulla salute o fitness
- Informazioni finanziarie
- Contenuti generati dall'utente

#### Dati raccolti da servizi di terze parti

Tuttavia, i servizi pubblicitari di terze parti integrati nella nostra App possono raccogliere automaticamente determinate informazioni. In particolare, **Google AdMob** può raccogliere i seguenti dati:

| Tipo di dati | Scopo | Raccolto da |
|--------------|-------|-------------|
| Identificatore dispositivo (IDFA) | Pubblicità personalizzata, tracciamento pubblicitario cross-app, misurazione delle prestazioni pubblicitarie | Google AdMob |
| Indirizzo IP | Prevenzione frodi, targeting geografico approssimativo, analisi | Google AdMob |
| Informazioni sul dispositivo | Ottimizzazione degli annunci, compatibilità del dispositivo | Google AdMob |
| Dati di utilizzo dell'app | Analisi, limitazione della frequenza degli annunci | Google AdMob |

**Importante**: Gli identificatori del dispositivo possono essere utilizzati per fornire annunci personalizzati su app e siti web e per misurare le prestazioni pubblicitarie. Questi dati vengono raccolti ed elaborati da Google, non da FlipFocus.

1. **Impostazioni iOS**: Vai su Impostazioni > Privacy e sicurezza > Tracciamento > Disattiva "Consenti alle app di richiedere il tracciamento"
